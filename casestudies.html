Design has always been my passion. I have designed in many disiplines, but have found the perfect fit in UX Design.
I have been programing since I was five years old and still love coming up with simple code solutions.
Whenever I get inspiration, or a new idea comes up in a meeting. I put the thought down on a index card and sort them by day.
I enjoy using graph paper and a square when I sketch a design. I also practice sketching a new icon daily.
I keep a rolodex of my favorite ideas and techniques to quickly refrence and ideate.
When manual drafting, I use tools and techniques learned as an architect to power my initial designs.
Memorizing hotkeys, creating custom macros, and loving to find and test new tools helps me enhance my productivity.
