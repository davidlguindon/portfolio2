  • Derek H.

  • Age: 26
  • Occupation: Dance Coordinator
  • Location: Chicago, IL
  • Gender: Male
  • Tier: Expert
  • Motivations: Derek loves teaching dance to children in the chicago area. He can easily lose track of time and often keeps his classes going a bit too long because he gets so involved in what he is doing. Derek also likes to make sure he is getting the proper amount of exercise in a day and likes to keep track of his calorie output while at work so he can stay healthy.
  • Goals:
      1. Help keep his classes timetable going smoothly with minimal effort
      2. Keep track of calorie intake and output
      3. Not needing to carry around a large device in the middle of dance class
  • Bio: Derek is a young, single working professional in a big city. He’s worked at a number of dance studios in Chicago, and is now leading running his own dance studio. While not afraid of technology,. He sees it more as a required tool that he does not want to think about often while going about his daily routine. He commutes into the city by bus. He’s interested in stamp collecting. He loves trying a new restaurant every night if possible. Quote: I want technology to make my life easier without distracting me from doing what I love”
