A LITTLE BIT ABOUT ME

I started going to college for civil engineering, got to my senior year and realized that engineering was not for me. While I enjoy problem solving and math, I have found over time that my true passion is design. So I switched my college focus to architectural design, and loved it. However, I could not afford to continue taking classes at my graduate school, and found myself always tinkering with UX design for fun. I decided to pivot to UX design. When I was in high school, I did a lot of UX design for a videogame my best friend and I made, and absolutely loved it. I spent more time repeatedly re sketching the interfaces and user flows for the game than I spent on any other task, including other all other school work combined. . I considered getting into programming after high school but, while I find coding interesting, it is not where my true passion rests. After twenty years of management experience, I am looking to work in a field that I find truly rewarding and am looking to work with a team that is focused on making life easier for the people around them through good UX design.
